Project NES

About the Project

Project NES is a homebrew NES game to represent the beginning of our studio! It is a lot harder than thought before though!

Game Features

  • Genre: Puzzle Escape
  • Platform: The NES and it's emulators
  • Core Mechanics: turn-based escape with enemies trying to gun you down.
  • Unique Levels: Designed for short bursts of gameplay (5 min per level). Something to pick up and get instantly, play then leave.
  • Themes: Going for gruff and badassery with the manual coming with the badass models I'm going for these characters
  • Character: You play as Vicky, who is in a mafia filled town, and as a rival boss she has to escape before they get to her.

Inspirations

  • Fire Emblem and an Itch.io game in the credits

Target Audience

Mostly just for new Soft Pretzel Studio fans to play some older games. also for anyone who may enjoy homebrew NES titles before we move on to the SNES
